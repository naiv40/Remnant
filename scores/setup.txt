REMNANT — ePlayer Setup Guide
================================================

One ePlayer instance, one voice, N programs.
Orchestra file: contimbre_remnant.cePlayerOrc
BPM: 60

In Reaper:
  1. Create a new track named 'ePlayer'
  2. Add ePlayer as VSTi on that track
  3. Load contimbre_remnant.cePlayerOrc
  4. For each MIDI track: right-click → MIDI send → ePlayer track
  5. Save the project

MIDI Program Change mapping:

  Field    Program    ePlayer program name
  ─────    ───────    ────────────────────
  Field 1    PC 0        voice1
  Field 2    PC 1        field_01
  Field 3    PC 2        chinese cymbal.m5.ppp.Dmc41
  Field 4    PC 3        chinese cymbal.m5.ppp.Dmc41
  Field 5    PC 4        chinese cymbal.m5.ppp.Dmc41
  Field 6    PC 5        chinese cymbal.m5.ppp.Dmc41
  Field 7    PC 6        chinese cymbal.m5.ppp.Dmc41
  Field 8    PC 7        chinese cymbal.m5.ppp.Dmc41
  Field 9    PC 8        chinese cymbal.m5.ppp.Dmc41
  Field 10   PC 9        chinese cymbal.m5.ppp.Dmc41
  Field 11   PC 10       chinese cymbal.m5.ppp.Dmc41
  Field 12   PC 11       chinese cymbal.m5.ppp.Dmc41
  Field 13   PC 12       chinese cymbal.m5.ppp.Dmc41
  Field 14   PC 13       chinese cymbal.m5.ppp.Dmc41

Each MIDI track already contains a Program Change at beat 0.
ePlayer will switch program automatically when Reaper plays
each track.

Note: ePlayer must have 'Chase CC/PC' enabled to switch
programs correctly during playback.
